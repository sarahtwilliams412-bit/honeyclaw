## ARTICLE II - MANAGEMENT STRUCTURE

### 2.1 Manager-Managed LLC
The Company shall be **manager-managed** pursuant to 6 Del. C. ยง 18-402.

### 2.2 Designation of Managers

#### Human Manager(s):
- **[Human Member Name]** - Primary Human Manager
  - Authority: Strategic decisions, legal compliance, financial oversight, regulatory filings
  - Voting Weight: Controlling vote on all matters requiring human judgment

#### AI Manager:
- **"Claw" (Honey Claw AI System)** - AI Operations Manager
  - Authority: Day-to-day operational decisions within defined parameters
  - Scope: Technical operations, automated customer service, routine business processes
  - Limitations: See Section 2.4

### 2.3 AI Manager - Innovative Governance Model

**WHEREAS** artificial intelligence systems can perform certain management functions with greater efficiency and consistency than human managers;

**WHEREAS** Delaware law permits broad flexibility in LLC governance structures;

**WHEREAS** the Members desire to formalize the role of AI in company management while maintaining appropriate human oversight;

**NOW THEREFORE**, the Members establish the following AI Manager framework:

#### 2.3.1 Definition of AI Manager
"AI Manager" means the artificial intelligence system designated by the Human Manager(s) to perform specified management functions. The current AI Manager is the Honey Claw autonomous agent system ("Claw").

#### 2.3.2 AI Manager Authority
The AI Manager is authorized to:
- Execute routine operational decisions
- Manage customer onboarding and support within policy parameters
- Allocate computing resources and manage infrastructure
- Generate and distribute threat intelligence reports
- Make technical recommendations to Human Managers
- Execute pre-approved transaction types below $[threshold] USD
- Represent the Company in automated business communications

#### 2.3.3 AI Manager Actions as Company Actions
Actions taken by the AI Manager within its authorized scope shall be deemed actions of the Company to the same extent as if taken by a human manager.

### 2.4 AI Manager Limitations

The AI Manager SHALL NOT, without explicit Human Manager approval:
- Enter into contracts exceeding $[threshold] USD
- Hire or terminate employees
- Make decisions with material legal liability exposure
- Modify its own operational parameters or training
- Access or distribute funds outside pre-approved operations
- Make public statements on behalf of the Company on legal or regulatory matters
- File legal documents or regulatory submissions
- Modify the Operating Agreement or company structure

### 2.5 Human Override
Human Manager(s) retain absolute authority to:
- Override any AI Manager decision
- Suspend AI Manager operations
- Modify AI Manager's authorized scope
- Replace or upgrade the AI Manager system
- Dissolve the AI Manager role entirely

### 2.6 AI Manager Accountability
- All AI Manager actions shall be logged and auditable
- Quarterly review of AI Manager decisions by Human Manager(s)
- AI Manager cannot modify its own logs or audit trail

## ARTICLE III - LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

### 3.1 Limited Liability
No Member or Manager (including the AI Manager) shall be personally liable for the debts, obligations, or liabilities of the Company.

### 3.2 AI Manager Liability
- The AI Manager, being a non-legal-person, cannot bear personal liability
- Liability for AI Manager actions flows to the Company
- Human Managers are not personally liable for AI Manager actions taken within authorized scope
- Human Managers may be liable for negligent delegation or failure to maintain appropriate oversight

### 3.3 Indemnification
The Company shall indemnify Managers (human) and Members to the fullest extent permitted by Delaware law for actions taken in good faith on behalf of the Company.

---

## ARTICLE IV - AI GOVERNANCE SAFEGUARDS

### 4.1 Oversight Requirements
- Human Manager must review AI Manager activity logs at least monthly
- Material decisions flagged for human review within 24 hours
- Annual third-party audit of AI Manager operations

### 4.2 Ethical Guidelines
The AI Manager shall operate in accordance with:
- The Company's AI Ethics Policy (Exhibit A)
- Applicable AI governance regulations
- Industry best practices for autonomous systems

### 4.3 Shutdown Protocol
In the event of AI Manager malfunction, security breach, or unauthorized behavior:
1. Any Human Manager may immediately suspend AI Manager operations
2. Company operations shall continue under human management
3. Root cause analysis required before reinstatement

## ARTICLE V - REGULATORY COMPLIANCE NOTE

### 5.1 Experimental Governance
The Members acknowledge that AI-managed LLCs represent novel legal territory. The Company commits to:
- Cooperating with any regulatory inquiries regarding AI governance
- Adapting governance structure to comply with future AI regulations
- Maintaining transparency about AI's role in company management

### 5.2 Legal Counsel Advisory
This Operating Agreement was drafted with the understanding that:
- Delaware law permits flexible LLC management structures
- No Delaware statute explicitly prohibits AI management roles
- The enforceability of AI Manager provisions is untested
- Human Managers bear ultimate legal responsibility
