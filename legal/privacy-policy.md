# Honey Claw Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 5, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 5, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Honey Claw ("we," "our," or "us") operates an AI-powered honeypot-as-a-service platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our service.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Email address (for account creation and communications)
- Authentication credentials (hashed, never stored in plaintext)
- Billing information (processed by third-party payment processors)

### 2.2 Honeypot Deployment Data
- Configuration settings for your honeypot instances
- Deployment logs and metadata
- Usage statistics (uptime, resource consumption)

### 2.3 Attacker Intelligence Data
- IP addresses of entities interacting with your honeypots
- Attack payloads, commands, and techniques captured
- Session recordings and behavioral patterns
- Malware samples submitted to honeypots

**Important:** Attacker data is collected from unauthorized access attempts to YOUR deployed honeypots. This data belongs to you and is processed on your behalf.

### 2.4 Automatically Collected Information
- Browser type and version
- Device identifiers
- Log data (access times, pages viewed)
- Cookies and similar tracking technologies

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the Honey Claw service
- Generate threat intelligence reports for your account
- Improve our AI models for better attack detection
- Send service-related communications
- Comply with legal obligations
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse of our platform

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- **Service Providers:** Cloud infrastructure, payment processors, analytics (under strict data processing agreements)
- **Aggregated Threat Intelligence:** Attack patterns may be anonymized and aggregated for the security community (no customer-identifiable data)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights
- **Business Transfers:** In connection with merger, acquisition, or asset sale

## 5. Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Honeypot Logs:** Retained according to your subscription tier (default: 90 days)
- **Attacker Data:** You control retention; exportable at any time

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- AES-256 encryption at rest
- TLS 1.3 encryption in transit
- Sandboxed honeypot environments (isolated from production systems)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- SOC 2 Type II compliance (in progress)

## 7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
- Export your data (portability)
- Opt out of certain processing
- Withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@honeyclaw.io

## 8. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in the United States. We use Standard Contractual Clauses for EU/UK data transfers where applicable.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Honey Claw is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
