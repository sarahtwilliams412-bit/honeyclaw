| **Amber** | `#D4790E` | Gradients, depth |

### Secondary Colors
| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Alert Red** | `#E74C3C` | Threat indicators |
| **Safe Green** | `#2ECC71` | Success states |
| **Terminal** | `#00FF41` | Code/hacker aesthetic accents |

### Dark Mode (Primary)
- Dark backgrounds (`#0D0D0D`, `#1A1A2E`)
- Honey gold as accent/highlight

## Typography Suggestions

### Wordmark Options
- **Sharp geometric sans:** Space Grotesk, Inter, DM Sans

- **Friendly but technical:** Outfit, Satoshi, General Sans

- **Logo/Headlines:** Space Grotesk Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular
- **Code/Technical:** JetBrains Mono
